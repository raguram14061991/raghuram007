About Me

I am a highway engineer and infrastructure design specialist with over 13 years of professional experience at the intersection of geometric design, computational modelling, and sustainable transport systems. My work integrates advanced corridor modelling in OpenRoads Designer with data-driven decision frameworks to optimise highway geometry, reduce earthwork quantities, and minimise lifecycle carbon impacts across complex projects in India and Denmark. Beyond conventional design practice, I approach transportation engineering as a systems problem—one that requires harmonising mobility efficiency, safety, environmental stewardship, and economic feasibility. My projects have consistently applied value engineering methodologies to improve constructability, optimise alignments, and enhance traffic performance while maintaining rigorous compliance with international standards.
